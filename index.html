Dr. Muhammad Aizzat Bin Zakaria

Head of Programme (Mechatronic) - UMP dual degree in collaboration with HsKA Germany / Researcher
Dr. Muhammad Aizzat Zakaria currently serves as a senior lecturer at the Faculty of Mechanical and Manufacturing Engineering, Universiti Malaysia Pahang (UMP) located in Pekan, Pahang, Malaysia. He is a research member of the Innovative Manufacturing, Mechatronics and Sports Laboratory (iMAMS). He is working under a sub-research cluster under Intelligent Robotics and Vehicles (IRoV) Laboratory which mainly focus on the intelligent vehicle development and mobile robotic solutions.
